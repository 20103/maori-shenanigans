What is a Powhiri?

A powhiri is a way of greeting visitors into a marae, and is done so when the visitors first arrive. It is also used for other places such as workplaces for welcoming a new employee.

5 Key stages of a Powhiri

  • Inoi (Prayer)



  • Wero (Challenge)



  • Karanga (Call)



  • Kai (Feast/Eating)



  • Koha (Gift)



The two main groups of a Powhiri

  • Tangata Whenua (Hosts)



  • The Manuhiri (Visitors)
